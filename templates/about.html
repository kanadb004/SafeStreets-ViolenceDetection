About SafeStreets

At SafeStreets, we are dedicated to leveraging cutting-edge technology to ensure the safety and security of women in public and private spaces. Our innovative service utilizes advanced Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) models to analyze CCTV footage in real-time, identifying and reporting instances of violence against women.

Our mission is to create a safer environment by providing timely alerts to authorities and concerned parties, enabling swift intervention and prevention of harm.

By combining the power of CNNs to detect visual patterns and LSTMs to understand temporal sequences, our system offers a robust solution for continuous monitoring and protection.

Join us in our commitment to making the world a safer place for women, one frame at a time.
